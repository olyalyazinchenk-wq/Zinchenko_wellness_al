# WellnessAI — Master Strategy

## North Star
We are building personal **Wellness Navigation in Telegram**: a service that turns
symptoms and lab results into a clear 8-week action plan, with live dialogue,
progress tracking, Russian-contour infrastructure and human-in-the-loop.

## Core Strategy
1. Enter market via a narrow, high-value wedge: Premium Wellness Dossier.
2. Deliver "wow" in dialogue: not a template, but a personal conversation with concrete steps.
3. Monetize cyclically: screening → dossier → monthly monitoring.
4. Build moat on Olga's knowledge base, UX quality and client dynamics data (not just LLM).

## USP (one line)
> За 15 минут превращаем непонятные анализы и жалобы в персональный,
> безопасный и выполнимый wellness-план с сопровождением в Telegram.

## Value
- **Client:** clarity, concrete steps, feeling of accompaniment.
- **Expert:** practice scales without quality loss.
- **Business:** recurring revenue, high loyalty, low ad dependency.

## Final Product Vision
1. Premium landing: calm medical-premium style, strong social proof, 1-click Telegram entry.
2. Telegram interface: live chat, "what's happening / what to do now" cards, soft follow-ups.
3. Client dossier: clean report (chat + PDF), 8-week roadmap, checkpoints.
4. Expert screen (internal): cases, red flags, AI draft, manual review, progress history.

## Non-Negotiable Principles
- Russian-contour infrastructure (Yandex Foundation Models) by default.
- Human-in-the-loop for any case-level recommendations.
- No diagnosis claims, no treatment promises, emergency red-flag escalation only.
- Quality measured by dialogue usefulness, not generic checklist output.

## Phased Plan
### Phase 1 — Dialogue Quality (7-10 days)
- Fix best model profile in Yandex.
- Test 30 real anonymized dialogues.
- KPI: personalization, usefulness, safety, latency.

### Phase 2 — Commercial Core (10-14 days)
- Screening → paid dossier → result delivery in chat.
- Payment integration and automatic delivery.
- KPI: conversion to payment, completed case rate.

### Phase 3 — Retention (2-3 weeks)
- Monthly monitoring, reminders, marker dynamics.
- KPI: repeat purchases/subscription, 30-day retention.

### Phase 4 — Expert Operations (3-4 weeks)
- Case queue, QA, expert edit templates, analytics.
- KPI: case turnaround time, final output quality, NPS.

### Phase 5 — Scale (after PMF)
- Partnerships, B2B mechanics, API/integrations.
