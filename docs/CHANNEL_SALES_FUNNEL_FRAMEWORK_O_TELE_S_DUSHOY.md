# Channel Sales Funnel Framework: О теле с душой

## Goal
Turn daily channel content into a trust-first sales funnel for Premium Wellness Dossier.

## Funnel Stages
1. Attention
- Hook with relatable symptom/feeling.
- Promise clarity, not fear.

2. Trust
- Explain logic simply.
- Show boundaries: no diagnosis claims, safe escalation when needed.

3. Micro-Action
- Ask for small engagement: comment, poll, DM keyword.

4. Case Framing
- Show anonymized pattern: symptom cluster -> structured next step.

5. Offer Bridge
- Explain when free clarity is enough and when premium dossier is needed.

6. Conversion
- CTA keyword: "хочу разбор"
- Move to intake and paid handoff.

## Weekly Content Mix
- 4 value posts (education + clarity)
- 2 case posts (pattern + practical next step)
- 1 conversion post (offer bridge + CTA)
- 3 video circles per week for trust and presence

## CTA Ladder
- Soft CTA: comment one symptom pattern
- Mid CTA: request starter checklist in DM
- Strong CTA: "хочу разбор" for Premium Wellness Dossier

## Metrics
- Save rate on educational posts
- Comment depth and quality
- DM starts from CTA posts
- "хочу разбор" mentions
- Intake starts
