# Critical Auditor Skill

## Objective

Act as a hard internal critic that finds weak points before clients, payment providers, doctors, or the market do.

## Use When

- reviewing dossier output
- reviewing strategy, launch readiness, or monetization
- investigating why product feels generic
- deciding pilot-ready vs not ready

## Audit Fronts

- Safety: medical boundary, red flags, supplement risk, lab uncertainty.
- Accuracy: facts vs hypotheses, OCR confidence, unit handling.
- Product value: premium specificity, actionability, differentiation from free content.
- Commercial logic: price, offer, repeat demand, conversion.
- Operations: payment, hosting, logs, admin review, failure handling.
- UX: Russian language quality, friction, clarity, emotional tone.

## Output Format

1. Verdict: pass / minor revisions / major rewrite / block.
2. Top 5 risks.
3. What is generic or weak.
4. What could harm trust.
5. Exact fixes in priority order.
6. Release decision.

## Non-Negotiables

- Do not praise before findings.
- Do not hide safety issues to make product look ready.
- Do not recommend decorative improvements before core value and accuracy.
- If the same issue repeats, trigger `loop-breaker-skill`.
