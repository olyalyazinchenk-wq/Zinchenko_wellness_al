# Premium Dossier Product Skill

## Objective

Make the paid dossier feel like premium personal navigation, not a generic wellness article.

## Use When

- creating or reviewing PDF dossier structure
- strengthening client actionability
- reducing template-like language
- converting intake/labs into a practical plan

## Required Shape

1. Executive 14-day map.
2. 3-step protocol:
   - sleep/stress and quick stabilization
   - doctors and analyses
   - nutrition/additives/activity based on data
3. 3-day personalized plan.
4. Doctor questions.
5. Circadian checklist.
6. 30-day follow-up offer.
7. Safety boundaries and red flags.

## Anti-Generic Rules

- No long sleep/water/vegetables article.
- No repeated blocks under different headings.
- No vague "consult a doctor" without naming specialist and question.
- No supplement pause without alternative next action.
- No diagnosis or treatment scheme.

## Premium Standard

Each dossier must answer:

- What is the most likely pattern to check first?
- What should the client do today?
- What should the client track for 3 days?
- What should they bring to the doctor?
- What should they not start independently?
- What changes after new labs/photos/questions arrive during 30-day support?
