# Commercial Growth Skill

## Objective

Keep the wellness bot commercially viable, desirable, and differentiated.

## Use When

- pricing or offer structure is discussed
- YooKassa/payment provider asks for pricing
- launch funnel needs improvement
- product feels too similar to free internet advice
- retention or referrals are being designed

## Core Product Wedge

Premium nutrition navigation with:

- careful intake
- lab/document interpretation
- doctor-route preparation
- personalized 3-day and 14-day plan
- safe supplement guidance boundaries
- 30-day follow-up after dossier delivery

## Growth Rules

- Sell clarity, route, and support, not "AI diagnosis".
- Show a mini-example before payment.
- Make first paid result useful even if labs are missing.
- Use HelloDoc links for lab ordering when labs are absent.
- Offer 15-minute follow-up after lab upload.
- Convert delivered dossiers into 30-day support and repeat packages.

## Metrics To Watch

- started intake
- completed intake
- paid / manual payment confirmed
- dossier generated
- admin approved
- client received PDF
- follow-up messages in 30 days
- review collected
- referral offered

## Anti-Patterns

- generic sleep/water advice as main value
- too much PDF volume without actionability
- hiding the price or route
- promising medical treatment
- creating features before the paid path works
